~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Results obtained in 2011 for UAL-version 4.08b
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

NOTE: For the particle diffusion coefficient D and pinch velocity v, the
values below correspond in fact to the individual coefficients for each
of the three transport models considered within ETS. For example, a v=1
translates into an effective pinch velocity of 3 in the density
transport equation and of 0+3/2+5/2=4 in the ion flux contributing to
heat transport.
